Target Generator

This tool generates a 8.5" x 11" PDF target for shooting. The target is designed to help you zero your scope and adjust for windage and elevation.

Instructions

Fill out the form below to generate a target PDF. The target will be generated with the following features:


 The number of MOA per click of your scope.

The yardage at which you will be shooting.

The thickness of the diagonal lines on the target. [inch]

Text to help you adjust your scope based on your shot group.
